About

Me

Atiya Akter Anisha (born April 2, 1999) is a celebrated Bangladeshi playback singer known for her soulful voice and dynamic vocal range. She earned national acclaim by winning the Bangladesh National Film Award for Best Female Playback Singer for her performance in the song "Ei Shohorer Pothe Pothe" from the 2022 film Payer Chhaap. With a growing legacy in the music industry, Anisha continues to captivate audiences through her passion, artistry, and powerful stage presence.

Career

Anisha's Musical Journey Anisha's journey into music gained momentum when she emerged as a finalist on the popular reality show Shera Kontho 2017. She further showcased her vocal talent on Zee Bangla Sa Re Ga Ma Pa, a renowned music competition loved by Bengali-speaking audiences. Her heartfelt rendition of "Behaya Mon", originally composed by Chishti Baul, resonated deeply with listeners and became a fan favorite. She began her playback singing career in 2019 and quickly rose to prominence in the Bangladeshi music scene. In recognition of her exceptional performance in the song "Cholo Niralay" from the film Poran, Anisha was honored with the Meril Prothom Alo Award (2022) and the Ananda Lahari Award, both celebrating her as the Best Female Playback Singer.
